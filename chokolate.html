HOLIDAY HOT CHOCOLATE

By Shuniaieva Marianna

Hot chocolate is a unique drink that will warm you up on a cold evening, wake you up in the morning, and just cheer you up at lunchtime. Preparing this drink is so easy, that even a child can handle this task. Having mastered our recipes, you will be able to delight yourself and your loved ones with this amazing drink, turning ordinary weekdays into small holidays.

    INGREDIENTS

  • milk 50ml
  • nstant coffee 0.5 tsp
  • Cinnamon to taste
  • Dark chocolate 50g.
  • Marshmallow marshmallows To taste

    PREPARATION

      Step 1
  • Add instant coffee to a cup of milk. We stir our mixture, pour milk into a saucepan and bring to a boil. You can also heat milk directly in the cup in which you mixed it with coffee. You can use a microwave for this.
      • Step 2
  • Chop the chocolate. For this, you can use a grater or an ordinary kitchen knife. Please note that in no case can substitutes be used to prepare this drink. That is, you will not be able to make hot chocolate from a chocolate bar. If you want to make a really tasty drink, you need bitter dark chocolate with a high cocoa content. Also, you should not use white or milk chocolate to make hot chocolate. Such chocolate has a rather low percentage of cocoa and you will simply spoil the products.
      • Step 3
  • Pour chopped chocolate into our hot milk. Leave the hot milk with chopped chocolate for a few minutes so that the chocolate melts and forms a homogeneous mass with the milk. Add a pinch of cinnamon to our drink. This spice will add a special aroma to our hot chocolate. However, if you don't like cinnamon, you can do without it.
      • Step 4
  • Mix our milk with chocolate until it becomes a homogeneous mass. Pay attention to the fact that the better the chocolate dissolves in milk, the darker our drink will become. Pour the finished hot chocolate into serving cups. We serve hot chocolate with marshmallow marshmallows.
